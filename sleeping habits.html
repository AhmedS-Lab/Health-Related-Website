Tips to improve your sleeping habits

Establishing healthy sleep habits, often referred to as sleep hygiene, is crucial for achieving restful and restorative sleep. Here are two key practices to consider:

Set a Consistent Sleep Schedule:

Aim to go to bed and wake up at the same time every day, including weekends. This regularity helps regulate your body's internal clock.









Avoid large meals, caffeine, and alcohol close to bedtime, as they can disrupt sleep. If you're hungry, opt for a light snack.
